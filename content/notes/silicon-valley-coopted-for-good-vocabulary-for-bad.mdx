They use words like _sharing_, _better_, _connection_ to sell products.

What for-good words are left that Silicon Valley haven't yet coopted? Radical, generosity, joy, compassion, gratitude.

These words are often anti-capitalist. Gratitude and generosity are particularly related to being ok with less.
